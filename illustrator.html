Zoo Project

I and a group were tasked on making icons for zoo exhibits. We each took an animal and I chose the lion. It had to be appealing to all age groups and distunguishable enough to know what they are. I was just getting started with illustrator and found my footing with this project. It was simple, yet complex enough for me to explore pens, brushes and ways to color on it.

Social Justice

With the social justice poster there was a lot of room to experiment. I chose domestic violence because people dont know that it's a problem that affects many people. The bars on the house represent how you can feel trapped in your own home, because vicims of abuse are often cut off from people they were close to and can't reach out to anyone about getting help. I played around with gradients in the sky and on the bars to make it look more metalic, and put a special texture on the X.

Word Project

This project was to pick a random word out of a hat, and illustrate it with the letters. My word was jumbled, and I tried multiple ideas before I started on this one. It took lots of patience entering in all of the individual shapes at the end, but I think it was worth it. It's the supposed to be the word jumbled up in a jar.
